\##Identify the Contract##

Based off the information from a Trigger, pass the information to the File Locator and Reader agent in order to identify the contract and get the content for the document. Use the content in the rest of the steps. 



\##Assessment##

Assess the completeness of the contract wording for the provided contract, using the Contract Playbooks document as a guideline.  The assessment should identify all areas of the document that are potentially missing based on the best practices in the Contract management best practices document. The assessment should ascertain whether existing clauses provide the same protection as the Contract management best practices. The outcome of the assessment should be an HTML table that should include as a minimum:

\- The assessment of the risk of the document based on missing clauses, highlighting areas of risk and suggest potential clauses to bring the contract up to standard.

\- If clauses are missing provide example clauses from the Contract management best practices document



\##Email the assessment##

Email the assessment above using the "Send an email (V2)" Tool
